About Us

We combine science, engineering and management to transform ideas into technological innovation.

Who are we?

EM Research & Tech is a private organization dedicated to research, technological development and consulting. Our mission is to promote applied innovation in electronic engineering, telecommunications, automation, control and the Internet of Things (IoT).

Established in Cusco, Peru, in November 2025, EM Research & Tech was created with a flexible structure focused on applied research, technical training, and technology transfer. Our team combines scientific expertise and entrepreneurial vision to build sustainable solutions with real impact.

Mission

To promote technological development and innovation through applied research, knowledge management and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Vision

To be a Latin American benchmark in technological innovation, promoting integration between science, engineering and society.
